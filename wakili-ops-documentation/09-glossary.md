# Glossary

## Advocate

A legal practitioner admitted to practice law in Kenya.

## Advocate Clerk

A staff member who supports filing, scanning, registry interaction, document preparation, diary management, and administrative follow-up.

## Audit Log

A record of important user and system actions, such as importing a document, changing status, exporting a filing pack, attaching a receipt, or restoring a backup.

## Backup Snapshot

A recoverable copy of the vault or selected matter data taken at a point in time. In this product, backups should be encrypted.

## Causelist

A list showing matters scheduled before a court or tribunal. Kenya Law publishes searchable cause lists received from court stations. See [Kenya Law Cause Lists](https://new.kenyalaw.org/causelists/).

## Cloud Backup

An optional encrypted backup stored with a cloud provider. In this product, cloud backup is not the primary storage location and should require explicit opt-in.

## Court Output

Any document or record generated by the court or court system, including orders, rulings, judgments, notices, receipts, hearing links, cause-list entries, and registry messages.

## Data Controller

An entity that determines the purpose and means of processing personal data. The ODPC handbook explains controller and processor roles. See [ODPC Personal Data Protection Handbook](https://www.odpc.go.ke/wp-content/uploads/2024/02/PERSONAL-DATA-PROTECTION-HANDBOOK.pdf).

## Data Processor

An entity that processes personal data on behalf of a data controller.

## DMS

Document Management System. In this documentation, the DMS is the Windows local-first system that stores and organizes legal documents.

## Document Classification

The process of assigning a type to a document, such as pleading, affidavit, annexure, order, receipt, authority, or correspondence.

## Document Version

A specific state of a document at a point in time, such as draft, signed copy, filed copy, amended copy, or served copy.

## E-Filing

Electronic filing of court documents through the Judiciary of Kenya e-filing system. The official portal is [efiling.court.go.ke](https://efiling.court.go.ke/).

## Encrypted Vault

The local protected document store where matter files and related metadata are kept.

## Filing Pack

A prepared set of court-ready documents and reports that an advocate or clerk manually uploads to the official e-filing portal.

## Filing Receipt

A court or portal-generated record confirming a filing or payment event.

## Local-First

A product design where documents remain on the user's device, external drive, or office storage by default. Internet services are optional and explicit.

## Matter

A client file, court case, transaction, dispute, advisory assignment, or other unit of legal work.

## Matter Vault

The local structured store of all documents, metadata, versions, court outputs, and filing packs for a matter.

## OCR

Optical Character Recognition. A process that extracts machine-readable text from scanned images or image-only PDFs.

## ODPC

Office of the Data Protection Commissioner in Kenya. ODPC administers and guides Kenya's data protection framework.

## PDF Readiness

The process of checking whether a document is suitable for court filing, including format, size, readability, encryption status, scan quality, and naming.

## RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation. An AI pattern where a system retrieves relevant source documents before generating an answer or draft.

## Restore Drill

A test that proves a backup can actually be restored.

## Scan Inbox

The staging area where newly scanned or imported documents wait for OCR, classification, matter matching, and user confirmation.

## Sensitive Matter

A matter requiring extra care because it contains sensitive personal data, protected persons, children, criminal allegations, family information, health information, or high-risk client data.

## Windows Legal Document Vault

The Windows plug-and-play local-first DMS, scanning, OCR, filing-pack, and backup product.

## Local Matter RAG Connector

The local RAG connector that indexes the DMS or existing document database without owning documents.

## Wakili-Mkononi Matter AI Integration

The Wakili-Mkononi integration that uses Local Matter RAG Connector to retrieve matter-scoped context for drafting and legal AI workflows.

## Version Session

A tracked episode in a document's lifecycle, such as review, signing, filing, correction, amendment, service, or archiving.

## Wakili-Mkononi

The existing Kenyan legal AI solution that can later consume local matter context through the RAG connector.
